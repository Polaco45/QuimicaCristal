Advanced Discounts & Loyalty with Customer Filters and Free Shipping

Features

This module extends Odoo's Discount & Loyalty features by introducing customer-specific filters for personalized discounts, country-based free shipping options, and seamless workflow integration. Designed to help businesses efficiently manage promotions and shipping policies, it provides tailored solutions to boost sales and customer satisfaction.

Create
Loyalty Customer Tier

  1. Go to your administration dashboard and navigate to the "Sales > Order > Loyalty Customer Tier" section.
  2. Click on the "Create" button, then provide a name and description for the loyalty tier.
  3. Save the record to finalize the creation of the loyalty customer tier.

Assign Loyalty Customer Tier

  1. Access the customer's profile in the administration panel.
  2. In the customer's details, locate the "Loyalty Customer Tier" field and select the appropriate tier from the dropdown menu.
  3. Save the changes to apply the assigned loyalty tier, which will determine their eligibility for specific discounts and customized promotions.

Custom Free Shipping

  1. Access your discount coupon configuration.
  2. Set the discount type to "Free Shipping".
  3. Define the eligible countries for the free shipping discount by specifying them in a one to many field.
  4. Save the settings to activate the free shipping discount for customers from the selected countries.

Free Shipping Reward

The free shipping coupon is effective only for customers whose shipping address matches the specified valid countries. Customers with addresses in those countries can use the coupon to receive free shipping, while others are not eligible for this benefit. Limiting the coupon to selected countries allows businesses to offer targeted promotions and control shipping costs.

Free Shipping Reward

When a country is not valid for a free shipping coupon, customers with shipping addresses in that country cannot use the coupon for free shipping.
