Benefits at a Glance

This module breaks the one-website barrier in Odoo. Effortlessly assign products and categories to multiple websites and streamline visibility across channels. With bulk update tools and intelligent listing logic, you’ll gain control, speed, and flexibility — exactly what serious eCommerce businesses need.

Multi-Website Product Visibility

Effortlessly manage product listings across multiple websites. By default, each product is visible on all sites — but you can fine-tune visibility per website as needed. A dedicated bulk update tool lets you adjust listings across your catalog in seconds.

Multi-Website Category Control

E-commerce Categories are inherently tied to product visibility. That’s why they also support multi-website configuration — with the same flexible controls and bulk-edit capabilities. Keep your product hierarchy consistent across sites, with precision.

Cross-Site Listing Manager

Get the full picture. The Centralized Products per Website Listing provides a unified interface to track and manage product visibility across websites. Gain precise control over listing status and lay the groundwork for advanced rules, segmentation, or channel-specific strategies.
